## Powerful Search Experience – A Key Propwrite Feature

The Propwrite Help Center showcases one of our platform's standout features: an intelligent and user-friendly search experience that helps users find answers in seconds.

<Image
  alt="Fuzzy search for a typo-tolerant search experience in the Propwrite Help Center"
  src="https://d2vwwcvoksz7ty.cloudfront.net/blog/cmdk-search.png"
  width={1607}
  height={946}
/>

Our search feature is typo-tolerant and searches across article titles, content, and anchor headings, ensuring that users can find what they need even if they're not sure of the exact terminology.

The best part: **This search functionality is available throughout your entire Propwrite-powered site**. Users can access it from any page using the keyboard shortcut `CMD+K` (or `CTRL+K` on Windows). Try it out on our site to see how it works!
